About GameDevHeroes

Our platform connects aspiring and professional game developers with industry experts to receive constructive feedback and guidance. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced developer, our experts are here to help you refine your skills and achieve your creative goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is GameDevHeroes?

GameDev Feedback is a platform designed to connect game developers with experts for feedback, mentorship, and advice on their projects.

Who are the experts?

Our experts are three graduate students specializing in areas like design, art, and programming.

How can I schedule a session?

You can schedule a session on our site and pick a contact method of your choosing: Disord, Zoom, Phone, etc.
